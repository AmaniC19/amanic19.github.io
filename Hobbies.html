League of Legends

League of Legends is my most current game that I play way to often for my own good. To describe in a nutshell, League of Legends is a MOBA game. MOBA meaning Multiplayer Online Battle Arena. League of Legends is held on an arena known as Summoner's Rift. Summoners Rift consist of 3 lanes: Top Lane, Middle Lane, and Bottom lane; as well as a Jungle sections of the map and river sections of the map. Summoner's Rift is where the central game is held. Players are put in a lane or jungle and choose their champion, or the characters that they can control on Summoner's Rift. After the 10 players pick their champions, because there is 5 people per team, and there are only 2 teams on the rift. There are 2 sides on Summoner's Rift, Red and Blue side. The blue is the left side and red is the right side. Then, the sides are reverse-flip of each other. For example, the red Jungle quadrant for the blue side, which is on the bottom side and where the red buff spawns, is on the top side for the red team. The same goes for the blue buff. You can learn more at the League of Legends Website. So now, League impacted me in terms of mindset and better myself. I feel like if I never got introduced into League, I wouldn't be who I am today. Also, I learned a decent bit of strategy, due it being a strategy 5v5 game. Also, I met a lot of cool people and actually have fun still playing this game and enjoying the competition and professional seen. Even if I'm a gold scrub, I enjoy the game and how it helps me better myself.
