Fairy Tail Origins

Fairy Tail originated with 4 members. These members were very powerful and their names are: Mavis, the guildmaster, Yuri Dreyar, Precht Gaebolg, and Warrod Sequen

Fairy Tail Main Members

Fairy Tail members consist of a lot of people. A few popular ones are: Mirajane Strauss, Lisanna Strauss, Elfman Strauss, Cana Alberona, Natsu Dragneel, Erza Scarlet, Gray Fullbuster, Lucy Heartfilia Wendy Marvell, and many many more.

Fairy Tail Plot

Fairy Tail's plot was about a girl named Lucy who really wanted to join Fairy Tail. She joined Fairy Tail and now is telling her stories of her time in Fairy Tail from her journal/diary.
